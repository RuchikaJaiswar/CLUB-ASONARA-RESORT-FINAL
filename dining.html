Dining
HIGH TIDE clearly communicates the value of The Resort as a brand by redefining fine dining with a fresh and contemporary ambience. With the capacity to seat 118 guests, this restaurant is planned in such a way that it creates different areas within the space provided to give an alternative experience to its patrons. It is located at the lobby level with a very contemporary mat finish and overlooks the pool & ocean, creating a welcoming light, airy and tranquil place to relax in, away from the heat of the day.
